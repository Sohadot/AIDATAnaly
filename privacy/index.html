Privacy - pre-launch policy

Privacy

This page describes how AIDAtanaly treats information entered into the reference site and scanner during the pre-launch phase.

What Data May Be Entered

The Transition Scanner may ask for funnel context (business model, goals, channels), measurement availability (whether analytics, CRM, or retention data exist), and self-assessed movement signals on T1-T4. These are diagnostic inputs, not identity records.

How Scanner Inputs Are Used

Scanner v1 computes governed output in the browser from your answers and from public /data/*.json registries. Pre-launch deployment does not require live analytics integrations. Results display on the scanner page; they are not published as separate indexable routes.

If server-side logging or report storage is introduced later, this page will be updated before indexed release to describe collection, retention, and deletion.

What Is Not Required

  • Account registration to use the reference scanner.
  • API keys or credentials to third-party analytics platforms.
  • Customer lists, payment data, or proprietary revenue figures.
  • Live CRM or ad platform connections for Scanner v1.

No Sensitive Data Requirement

The scanner should not require sensitive personal data. Do not enter government identifiers, health information, financial account numbers, passwords, or other regulated personal data into scanner fields. Use business-level and funnel-level descriptions only.

Future Report Handling

If you request an ATI Snapshot or paid diagnostic brief, any contact or delivery details will be handled under an updated privacy notice published before that feature is indexed. Export features must not require unnecessary personal data.

Contact or Inquiry Path

For privacy questions before public launch, use the contact channel published on the site at launch or the inquiry path defined in Governance communications. Public pages are indexable after governed release activation; scanner inputs remain optional and must not require sensitive personal data.
