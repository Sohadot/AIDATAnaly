Core Diagnostic Question

Did curiosity become preference?

Definition

T2 measures whether engagement crystallizes into preference, intent, motivation, or commercial desire. It governs the transition from curiosity to choice pressure — the point where a prospect stops browsing and starts wanting a specific outcome from a specific offer.

Preference and intent are sub-signals inside T2, not separate transition vectors.

Why This Transition Matters

Interest without desire is passive consumption. Content metrics, session counts, and educational traffic can look strong while commercial intent never forms. T2 is where engagement either becomes preference or remains entertainment. Weak T2 produces funnels full of curious visitors who never develop the motivation to act.

Strong Signals

  • Product comparison
  • Pricing exploration
  • Repeat content consumption
  • Feature-specific engagement
  • Brand-specific search
  • Saved product or wishlist behavior
  • Demo page exploration
  • Review consumption
  • Return visits with deeper intent
  • Sales or product inquiry behavior

Weak Signals

  • Passive reading
  • Non-commercial engagement
  • Repeat visits without deeper movement
  • Comparison without preference
  • High content consumption with no intent signal
  • Educational traffic with no offer relevance
  • Weak differentiation

Evidence Confidence Considerations

T2 requires intent signals beyond page views: pricing visits, comparisons, inquiries, or CRM-stage progression. Self-reported belief that "people are interested" without observable intent behavior yields Evidence Confidence E1 and provisional language. Multi-source evidence (analytics plus CRM) supports E3 readings.

Failure Modes Under This Vector

When T2 movement weakens, the Transition Failure Ontology classifies it into one of five governed failure modes:

Passive Engagement

tfo.t2.passive_engagement

Engagement without commercial intent formation.

Comparison Stall

tfo.t2.comparison_stall

Evaluation without preference resolution.

Preference Dilution

tfo.t2.preference_dilution

Interest without preference for this offer.

Intent Evaporation

tfo.t2.intent_evaporation

Early intent fades before desire solidifies.

Value Ambiguity

tfo.t2.value_ambiguity

Surface understanding without decisive value.
